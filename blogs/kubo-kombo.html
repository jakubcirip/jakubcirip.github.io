Motivation and Idea

First of all, I would like to offer you an insight into what motivated me for this project.

Why would someone want a guitar combo?
The answer is simple, they want to play their guitar with a decent sound.
But why not just buy one, but make one? - I like to make simple things more complicated;)
The basic idea was to use 2 45W speakers that I had left over from the original setup.
