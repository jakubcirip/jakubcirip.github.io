Motivation and Idea

First of all, I would like to offer you an insight into what motivated me for this project.

Why would someone want a guitar combo?
The answer is simple, they want to play their guitar with a decent sound.
But why not just buy one, but make one? - I like to make simple things more complicated;)
The basic idea was to use 2 45W speakers that I had left over from the original setup.

In the next section we will see the assembly of components for the project.

List of compoments

  • 2x 45W 8Ω reproductors
  • MeanWell LRS-150-24, Power supply 24V, 6.5A, 150W
  • 2x Preamplifier Tone Board, Hi-Fi Audio Amplifier Module DC 12–24V
  • Audio stereo amplifier HX-M189 - 2 x 50W
  • 2x JFET buffer
  • Combo case made of plastic, wood and damping material

Kombo Design - proposal and solution

The dimensions must be designed so that all the modules of the combo are placed inside it and are easily accessible for assembly and serviceability.

The dimensions of the box are:
420 x 320 x 230

The 420 width is sufficient to comfortably place two 185 diameter speakers side by side.
In the middle of the box and on the sides I designed various points for attaching all the components and modules.
