About Me

I am a dedicated and passionate Linux kernel developer and system administrator with a strong background in computer networks and mechatronics.


I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Networks from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Informatics at the Technical University of Košice (TUKE). Later, I completed a Master's degree in Mechatronics from the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Košice (SJF).


Currently, I work as a Linux Administrator at Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions Slovakia in Košice. My role involves managing and optimizing Linux-based systems, troubleshooting complex network and server issues, and ensuring the stability of critical infrastructure.


I am also actively involved in automating processes and optimizing system performance, contributing to the efficient and reliable operation of our IT services.


In my spare time, I focus on deepening my knowledge of Linux kernel development and enhancing my skills in low-level programming with C/C++. I am also passionate about open-source projects and contributing to the community whenever I can.
