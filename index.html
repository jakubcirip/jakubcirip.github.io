About Me

I am a dedicated and passionate Linux kernel developer and system administrator with a background in computer networks, programming and mechatronics.


I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Networks from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Informatics at the Technical University of Košice (FEI TUKE). Later, I completed a Master's degree in Mechatronics from the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Košice (SjF).


Currently, I work as a Linux Administrator at Deutsche Telekom IT Solutions Slovakia in Košice.


In my spare time, I focus on deepening my knowledge of Linux kernel development and enhancing my skills in low-level programming with C/C++.
